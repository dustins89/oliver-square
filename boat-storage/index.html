Boat Storage FAQ

What's the largest boat that fits?

Our large bays (60 ft deep × 14.5 ft wide × 10 ft door) accommodate most trailered pontoons, bass boats, ski boats, cuddy cabins, and deck boats up to about 28 ft total length (including the trailer tongue). Width is not usually a limiting factor for trailered boats — the 10-ft door height is the constraint, which fits most trailered boats with the exception of some tall cruisers or boats with tall towers.

Can I do my own winterization on-site?

Yes. Our bays have LED lighting and electrical service, so you can fog the engine, drain lines, change oil, shrink-wrap, or do any DIY winterization in your bay without hauling to a marina. Save marina service fees every year.

How far is Oliver Square from local lakes?

Oliver Square is centrally located for boaters using the Youghiogheny River Lake (~25 min), Cheat Lake (~35 min), Lake Lynn (~25 min), Dunlap Creek Lake, and Monongahela River access points. You're closer to your rig than a lakeside slip at a fraction of the cost.

Is there a hose or wash rack?

No wash rack inside the bays. Our focus is secure weather-protected storage — for hull rinsing, plan to do it before bringing the boat back to the bay.

Can I pick up my boat before dawn for a fishing trip?

Absolutely. Pin pad keyless access means you can pull in and pull out any time, day or night. Our site-wide security camera system keeps an eye on things while you're away.
