RV Storage FAQ

Will my Class A motorhome fit at Oliver Square?

No. Most Class A motorhomes are 12–13 feet tall, and our door opening is 10 feet. Class A RVs, large Class C motorhomes with rooftop AC, and most 5th-wheel trailers will not clear the door. Class B camper vans, smaller Class C motorhomes, travel trailers, and pop-ups generally fit fine. Call 412-246-4662 with your specific model and we'll verify clearance.

How long can I store my RV?

Month-to-month for seasonal storage, or long-term annual leases. Many of our RV customers store Oct–Apr when they're not using the rig; others keep a bay year-round as permanent off-site parking. Multi-bay and multi-year discounts available.

Can I plug my RV into the bay electrical?

Yes — each bay has a standard 120V outlet. This is ideal for a battery tender or trickle charger to keep your coach batteries topped up through winter storage. (Not a full 30/50A RV shore power hookup.)

How do I get my RV in and out after hours?

Every bay has pin pad keyless entry. Pull straight up, enter your code, automatic door opens, drive in. Leave for a pre-dawn trip or come home late from a weekend without coordinating with an office.

Is there water or sewer dump service?

No. Our bays are indoor vehicle storage — no hookups for water, sewer, or 30/50A shore power. For full-service RV parks, you'll want a campground. For off-season secure indoor storage that protects your investment, we're your spot.
