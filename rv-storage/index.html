What Fits in Our RV Storage Bays

Oliver Square's 10-ft door opening and 60-ft-deep large bays accommodate most smaller recreational vehicles in Uniontown and Fayette County, PA.

✓ Fits Comfortably

  • • Class B camper vans (Airstream Interstate, Winnebago Travato, Roadtrek, Mercedes Sprinter-based)
  • • Small Class C motorhomes under 10 ft tall
  • • Travel trailers up to ~30 ft long, under 10 ft tall
  • • Pop-up campers, teardrop trailers, A-frames
  • • Truck campers (slide-in)
