Leasing FAQ

What types of commercial space are available at Oliver Square?

Oliver Square has 20 retail and office suites in the main plaza building, ranging from smaller storefronts to full office suites up to 3,300 sq ft. Every suite has storefront-style access, on-site parking directly in front, and signage rights on the shared pylon.

What tenant mix is already at Oliver Square?

Restaurants, salons and spas, family entertainment, professional services, healthcare, dance studios, and specialty retail — a diverse complementary mix that drives steady daily traffic across the plaza.

How visible is Oliver Square from the road?

Oliver Square sits directly on Oliver Road with Route 51 frontage in Uniontown, PA. The plaza shares a large pylon sign visible to all passing traffic, and each suite has individual facade signage rights. We're also just minutes from the PA Route 43 (Mon-Fayette Expressway) exit — a major regional commuter corridor from Pittsburgh to Morgantown.

What are the lease terms?

Flexible lease terms tailored to your business — typically 3 to 5 years with renewal options, NNN or gross structures depending on the suite. Tenant improvement allowances are negotiable case-by-case. Call 412-246-4662 or email info@3riversrentals.com to discuss your specific needs.

Is there ample parking at Oliver Square?

Yes. Oliver Square has a large shared parking lot directly in front of the retail plaza, accommodating customers of all 20 retail suites plus tenant employees. Handicap-accessible spots are available at the storefront entrances.

Can I also rent storage or contractor space in addition to a retail suite?

Yes. Oliver Square also has 20 secure storage bays and contractor bays behind the retail plaza. Many of our tenants lease both a retail suite out front and a storage/contractor bay in back for inventory, vehicles, or overflow. Bundled discounts available — see Contractor Bays.
