Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about renting storage units and retail space at Oliver Square.

How much does storage cost at Oliver Square?

Large bays (870 sq ft) start at $600/month. Standard bays (580 sq ft) start at $400/month. All bays include automatic doors, LED lighting, electrical service, pin pad access, and site-wide security cameras. Long-term and multi-bay discounts available — call 412-246-4662 or email info@3riversrentals.com.

What is the largest vehicle I can store at Oliver Square?

Our large bays (Units 1-11) are 60 feet deep by 14.5 feet wide with a 10-foot door opening. They fit most boats on trailers, Class B camper vans, work trucks with trailers, long-bed vehicles, motorcycles, and smaller travel trailers. Class A motorhomes and most fifth-wheel trailers exceed 10 feet tall and will not fit — please call 412-246-4662 to confirm clearance for your specific vehicle.

Do you offer climate-controlled storage?

Our storage bays are not climate-controlled, but they are fully enclosed and weather-protected. Electrical service is provided for items such as battery tenders and trickle chargers. For safety, space heaters, dehumidifiers, and open-flame devices are not permitted inside storage bays.

How do I access my storage unit after hours?

Every storage bay has pin pad keyless access, so you can reach your unit anytime. Our site-wide security camera system monitors the property 24/7 for added peace of mind.

Do you have storage units available for rent in Uniontown, PA?

Yes. Oliver Square offers 20 secure storage units and contractor bays for rent at 6 Oliver Road, Uniontown, PA 15401. Every bay features automatic doors with openers, LED lighting, electrical service, pin pad access, and is covered by our site-wide security camera system. Call 412-246-4662 for current availability and pricing.

Do you offer boat, RV, or car storage in Uniontown, PA?

Yes. Our storage bays are well-suited for boat storage, Class B camper van storage, motorcycle storage, classic car storage, and smaller travel trailer storage. Door height is 10 feet — Class A motorhomes and most fifth-wheel trailers will not fit. Each bay includes an automatic door with opener, LED lighting, electrical service for battery tenders, pin pad access, and is covered by site-wide security cameras.

What security features does Oliver Square have?

Our entire site is covered by a security camera system, and every storage bay is accessed via pin pad for keyless entry and a full audit trail of access. No shared keys, no lost keys.

What can I store in an Oliver Square contractor bay?

Contractor bays are ideal for tools, building materials, work vehicles, trailers, landscaping equipment, small business inventory, seasonal storage, and boat/RV/car storage. Every bay features automatic doors, LED lighting, electrical service, and pin pad access — functional enough to double as a light workshop.

Do you offer month-to-month storage leases?

Yes, we offer flexible month-to-month and long-term lease options to accommodate both short-term and ongoing storage needs.
